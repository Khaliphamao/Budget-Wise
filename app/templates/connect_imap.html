Connect Your Email via IMAP

Budget Wise can connect directly to your email using IMAP to scan for transaction emails.

How it works:
  1. We connect to your email via IMAP to scan for transaction alerts from Nigerian banks and fintechs
  2. We extract transaction details like amount, date, and description from these emails
  3. We categorize and organize them in your dashboard
  4. Your password is not stored in our database for security reasons
Your password will be used to connect to your email but won't be stored.
Common IMAP servers:
  • Gmail: imap.gmail.com
  • Yahoo: imap.mail.yahoo.com
  • Outlook: outlook.office365.com
Note for Gmail users: You'll need to enable "Less secure app access" or use an "App Password" if you have 2-factor authentication enabled. Learn more
